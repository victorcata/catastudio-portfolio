UX DESIGN AND FRONT-END DEVELOPMENT

In my first six years working as a Microsoft .NET Back-End developer, and working in almost every phase of software development, I realized that most of the development that I was doing could be executed in the client instead of the server. So, I started to move part of my Back-End development to the Front-End.

Thanks to my capacity to moving between different stacks development my company Avanade offered me a position in the UX department. Since 2015, I have been working as a Front-End developer and User experience designer, mainly as an Interaction Designer. I've been working for many top clients via Avanade in Spain doing their websites. Also, thanks to my experience as a Back-End developer I've been very helpful in the future integration of my Front-End website with the Back-End solution. Below are some examples of recent projects:
